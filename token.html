Royal Token

ROYAL token is the native currency in the game. It allows token holders to play, invest and also be part of the game.

Token Functionality

Purchase Game Items

ROYAL can be used to purchase in-game assets,Such as palaces, castles, military equipment and weapons

Play to earn

Users can earn ROYAL tokens through participation in different in-game events, such as competitions

Staking Rewards

ROYAL token can be used in the system to earn staking rewards. It is designed to encourage users to hold tokens and support the ecosystem.

Governance

Token holders could participate in the decentralized governance process that decides on the use of revenues, game development and growth.

FAQs
This section includes answers to frequently asked questions concerning Mobile ROYAL

What governance elements will be enabled in ROYAL Token?
MOBILE ROYAL will establish a decentralized organization called Community Council governed by the community. Everyone who owns ROYAL tokens will be able to join the Community Council and participate in the governance process by voting, proposing improvements and deciding on the budget.
What DeFi features does MOBILE ROYAL include?
One of our strategic visions within MOBILE ROYAL is to create monetary incentives for the most committed and motivated players. The key DeFi elements in the game include: 

NFT:s, or non-fungible tokens, presented in the form of in-game items, such as palaces, castles, military equipment and weapons and others. Players will be able to stake their , ROYAL tokens and get a staking yield 

NFT collateral. When a user buys NFT from the system, a percentage of the purchase price might go into a special collateral account associated with NFT. User then has an option to sell it back to the system (i.e. destroy the NFT) for the price equivalent to the locked collateral (or a percentage of it). This guarantees that the value of NFT cannot go below a certain threshold.
Will I need to have a Crypto wallet to play the game?
No, you will not be asked for a crypto wallet to get onboarded and play the game. In MOBILE ROYAL , only you will decide on how much you want to be involved in the game's decentralized features and DeFi. At the same time, if you would decide to join the Community Council or purchase different in-game items, like NFTs, you will require a cryptocurrency wallet to make a transaction.
